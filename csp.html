‚ë§ Boolean Expressions: Boolean values are either true or false, often represented as "1" (true) or "0" (false). Common Boolean operators include AND, OR, and NOT, frequently used in conditionals and loops.

‚ë® Developing Algorithms: Algorithms can be developed from an idea, by combining existing algorithms, or by modifying current solutions. These sequences of steps solve specific problems and often incorporate patterns for efficiency.
